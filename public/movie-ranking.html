Top Movies Of All Time According To Me

My Best 3 Movies Of All Time

Jersey (2019)

Genre: Sports Drama
Director: Gowtam Tinnanuri
"Jersey" is an emotional rollercoaster about a struggling cricketer trying to prove himself one last time – for his son. Nani’s heartfelt performance, along with an inspiring storyline, makes it a memorable watch. The songs, especially "Adhento Gaani Vunnapaatuga", perfectly complement the mood of the film. It’s a story of passion, failure, and the love that keeps us going.

Arjun Reddy (2017)

Genre: Romance, Drama
Director: Sandeep Reddy Vanga
"Arjun Reddy" is an intense and raw portrayal of love, heartbreak, and self-destruction. Vijay Deverakonda’s performance brought a fresh wave to Telugu cinema with his bold and realistic acting. The cinematography and background score amplify the emotional depth, while songs like "Inkem Inkem" and "Madhurame" became chartbusters. It’s a film that broke stereotypes and sparked conversations across the country.

Sita Ramam (2022)

Genre: Romantic Drama
Director: Hanu Raghavapudi
"Sita Ramam" is a beautiful and timeless love story set against the backdrop of war and patriotism. The film follows the emotional journey of Lieutenant Ram, an orphaned army officer, whose life changes after receiving anonymous love letters from Sita Mahalakshmi. The storytelling is poetic, layered with emotions, surprises, and elegance. Dulquer Salmaan and Mrunal Thakur deliver stellar performances, and the visuals are simply mesmerizing. With a soulful soundtrack by Vishal Chandrashekhar ("Inthandham", "Oh Sita Hey Rama"), the film leaves a lasting impression and has been praised as one of the best romantic Telugu films of recent times.
